Project on Console

To download and set up visual studio you have to do the commands shown. First go to https://code.visualstudio.com/download. Then press download on your device. Then open Visual Studio and open a empty file. After that you open 3 new files and say style.css, main.js and index.html.

The use of console screen is to test. If ou want try a piece of code you can use the console screen. To do this first go to the puzzle piece on the left side of the screen. When you click on it search live server, then downloading it. After that then you click on the puzzle again. Then click the gear, after that click extension settings. Then go down to live server and chose Google Chrome. After that you go back to the index.html screen. Then you can see a little radar at the bottom right side corner and it says go live. Then you click it and it will open a tab in Chrome. Then you right-click and press inspect then pressing console and you can see commands. Declare a variable and then do console.log() and then you can see your commands.
